About MediCare Clinic

MediCare is a modern clinic management system built to digitize appointment booking, patient records, and doctor scheduling.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, efficient, and paperless healthcare administration — eliminating long queues, lost records, and scheduling conflicts.

Features
  • Online patient registration and authentication
  • Appointment booking with real-time slot management
  • Doctor schedule viewing and management
  • Appointment approval and cancellation workflow
  • Patient medical history records
  • Admin management dashboard
  • Contact and feedback form
Technology Stack

Built with Django Framework, SQLite database, Bootstrap 5, and Django Templates — as part of CSC 1202 Software Development Project, Makerere University, 2026.
